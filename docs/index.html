myDenverCard

The myDenverCard is a free pass issued by the City of Denver that gives youth free admission to museums, the zoo, botanic gardens, recreation centers, and pools across the city.

Who qualifies

  • Denver residents in kindergarten through 12th grade
  • Ages 5 to 18
  • The card is free to sign up for
Get a free myDenverCard

For older adults

Denver residents ages 60+ qualify for myDenverPrime, a free membership to all Denver rec centers and pools. Sign up in person at any rec center with proof of age and Denver residency.
